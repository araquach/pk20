Schwarzkopf Professional have revamped the Vibrance range and it is a game changer! They have developed a moisturising technology that is in all of their vibrance colours. This technology hydrates the hair during the colouring process and protects the hair from common damages that can occur during colouring.

![Vibrance Looks](https://99119d29eb658e74481c-40e51394fe766397114262205a4ab22d.ssl.cf5.rackcdn.com/blogs/New%20Vibrance/vibrance_looks.jpg)

They have developed a fabulous new clear gloss which creates ultimate shine and smoothness. This can be added to your colour or used on its own to refresh your locks inbetween appointments.

![Vibrance Bottles](https://99119d29eb658e74481c-40e51394fe766397114262205a4ab22d.ssl.cf5.rackcdn.com/blogs/New%20Vibrance/vibrance_bottles.jpg)

There are now 68 unique shades ranging from vibrant coppers to soft pastels. With the new mix tones, your stylist now has even more capability to tailor make and create the perfect tone for your look!
