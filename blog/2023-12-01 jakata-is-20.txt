Twenty years ago, a vision took root as JAKATA Salon opened its doors in a modest location, marking the beginning of a remarkable journey in the heart of Warrington. Over the years, this visionary establishment has evolved into an iconic symbol of excellence in hairdressing, marked by its dedication to innovation, commitment to a diverse clientele, and most importantly, the incredible clients and team that fuels its success.

![THE WHOLE TEAM](https://35fba853288929fc2c78-69cb25cc8c90ed23a3699fb2b2ac841c.ssl.cf5.rackcdn.com/JAKATA%20is%2020/JAK%20-%20Blog%20Images.png)

JAKATA's story is one of transformation and growth. From its initial inception to its current establishment and the birth of sister salons, 'Paul Kemp Hairdressing' and 'Base Hairdressing Training Academy,' the journey has been nothing short of inspiring. The original spot of JAKATA now houses 'Base Hairdressing Training Academy,' a testament to the continuous commitment to nurturing the next generation of talented hairdressers.

At the core of JAKATA's success lies the unparalleled dedication and expertise of its team, fondly referred to as the 'OGs' - the long-standing members who have been the backbone of the salon since its early inception. Their unwavering passion, skill, and commitment have not only shaped the salon's legacy but also inspired and mentored the newer members joining the fold.

JAKATA owes its enduring success to its loyal and diverse clientele, whose unwavering support has been the driving force behind two decades of excellence. The recent celebration at 'Hideout' in Warrington was a heartfelt thank you to our clients, partners, and the entire team, commemorating 20 years of shared milestones and cherished memories.

What sets JAKATA apart from the rest is not just its commitment to innovation but the exceptional leadership steering this vision into reality. At the helm stands Adam, the salon's owner, whose unwavering belief in the power of people has been the guiding light for the team. With a laid back attitude, a penchant for tech and a thoughtful, innovative approach, Adam's methodical strategies have propelled JAKATA into new realms of success.

In line with JAKATA'S commitment to grooming future talent, Base Hairdressing Training Academy offers two exceptional schemes: the Pre-Apprenticeship Scheme for those still in education seeking a taste of the hairdressing world, and the Apprenticeship Scheme, a pathway for individuals looking to build their careers in this dynamic industry. The open-door policy and emphasis on feedback and collaboration reflect JAKATA's ethos of continuous improvement. The team training and social events, including the recent attendance at the British Hairdressing Awards, underscore the salon's commitment to nurturing a vibrant and socially engaged team.
