## THE RANGE

The range is made up of **four lines**, each creator curated their own line with an additional general care line. There are a total of **14 products**, ranging from shampoos and conditioners, pomades, sprays and powders. Long or short hair, whatever your styling needs, there is definetly a product from STMNT for you!

## STMNT CARE

The STMNT Care line is made up of 5 products, shampoo, conditioner, beard oil, all-in-one cleanser and a hair and body cleansing bar. All are developed to care for every hair and beard type, infused with activated charcoal to purify and menthol for a minty fresh feeling.
