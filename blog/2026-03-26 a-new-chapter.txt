There comes a point in every brand’s journey where growth calls for change and after years of building trusted client relationships, delivering consistent results and growing a talented team, we’re proud to share that Paul Kemp Hairdressing is evolving into Sloane the Salon.

Sloane felt right from the beginning, refined, confident and style aware. It's a name that the reflects the standard of work we deliver and the clients we're proud to welcome through our doors. 

Even the culture around the name has long been associated with fashion, hair and timeless style. And sometimes, the right name finds you. As the space came together, we realised our new flooring carries the name Sloane. A small detail, but one that felt quietly aligned.

It simply felt like the right step forward and this change reflects where we are now and where we’re heading.

# What Stays the Same

What has always mattered most hasn't changed.

- The team you know and trust.
- The standards we work to.
- The time we take to listen.
- The results you leave with.

Our clients have always been at the heart of everything we do and the trust you place in us carries forward into this next chapter.

# A Refreshed Space

Alongside our new name, the salon has undergone a full refit.

The space was in need of an upgrade and has been thoughtfully redesigned to feel cleaner, calmer and more aligned with the experience we want to offer, elevated and welcoming.

The atmosphere feels refreshed. The energy renewed. And for our clients, the feeling of walking out confident remains exactly as it should be.

![Sloane Section](https://35fba853288929fc2c78-69cb25cc8c90ed23a3699fb2b2ac841c.ssl.cf5.rackcdn.com/A%20New%20Chapter/Sloane%20-%20Section.jpg) 

# A Natural Evolution

This transition reflects growth and the heart of the salon remains exactly where it has always been, in the hands of a talented team dedicated to delivering exceptional hair and Sloane is simply the next expression of that commitment.

*We’d like to sincerely thank our clients for your patience and support while the refurbishment has taken place, your understanding has meant a great deal to us and we can’t wait to welcome you back through our doors and into the refreshed Sloane space.*
