## Blonde Wonders Golden Mask 
This mask is suitable for all blondes looking for a haircare product to achieve luxuriously shiny hair, either as an upgrade to your colour service or as a stand-alone salon service in between colour appointments.

### What does it do?
Helps to re-bond hair while illuminating all blonde hair types.
The unique formula has gold particles that transforms from a cream to paste during use, brightening and illuminating the hair with no colour shift. The 3D Bonding Technology also strengthens the inner hair structure by up to 95%. 

### Is the Blonde Wonders Golden Mask vegan?
Yes, the Golden Mask is vegan and also free from silicone oils, instead the formula contains plant-based Apricot Kernel Oil.

### Does Golden Mask affect the client’s hair colour?
No, there will be no colour shift. In fact, the Golden Mask will improve light reflection.

Should the Golden Mask be applied to towel dry hair only?
No, it can be applied to dry, brushed/detangled hair as well. ​

### Which hair types is Golden Mask ideal for?
Slightly damaged to highly porous, highlighted, full head bleached or high-lift colour, balayage​d hair.​
