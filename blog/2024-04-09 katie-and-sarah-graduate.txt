In the world of hairdressing, every snip, every curl, and every client interaction holds within it the promise of transformation. It's a journey that requires not only skilful hands but also boundless creativity and a heart brimming with passion. 

![Katie and Sarah picture](https://35fba853288929fc2c78-69cb25cc8c90ed23a3699fb2b2ac841c.ssl.cf5.rackcdn.com/Katie%20and%20Sarah%20graduate/JAK%20-%20Blog%20Page%20Meta%20(4).png)

Meet **Katie and Sarah**, two remarkable individuals who have honed their craft at **Base Hairdressing** Academy. Now, they're ready to spread their wings and fly the nest from Base, making their mark at **Jakata Salon**, our longest-standing sister salon. Armed with their expertise and boundless enthusiasm, they're set to weave magic into every strand of hair they touch.

At **Base Hairdressing Academy**, under the expert guidance of Layla, the Salon Manager & Head of Education, and Vikki, the Head of Training, Katie and Sarah underwent rigorous training that moulded them into the stylists they are today. Their journey was marked by countless hours of practice, learning the latest techniques, and mastering the art of exceptional customer service. But beyond the technical skills, what truly sets Katie and Sarah apart is their unwavering dedication and their infectious personalities that light up the salon floor.

![Katie picture](https://35fba853288929fc2c78-69cb25cc8c90ed23a3699fb2b2ac841c.ssl.cf5.rackcdn.com/Katie%20and%20Sarah%20graduate/JAK%20-%20Blog%20Page%20Meta%20(6).png)

**Katie**, a passionate stylist with a flair for creativity, brings a dash of Sagittarius determination to her craft. Her journey into hairdressing began amidst a media makeup course, but her heart swiftly swayed towards hair, where she found her true calling. With an avant-garde hair up award under her belt and a knack for motivating others, Katie's artistic flair shines brightly as she creates unforgettable transformations for her clients. Her infectious enthusiasm and genuine care for others, coupled with her witty humour, make her a cherished member of the team, and her clients adore her for the joy and confidence she brings to every appointment.

![Sarah picture](https://35fba853288929fc2c78-69cb25cc8c90ed23a3699fb2b2ac841c.ssl.cf5.rackcdn.com/Katie%20and%20Sarah%20graduate/JAK%20-%20Blog%20Page%20Meta%20(5).png)

**Sarah**, on the other hand, is the epitome of welcoming, relaxed, and cool, just like your favourite playlist. With her easygoing nature and natural talent for hairstyling, Sarah has endeared herself to both clients and colleagues alike. Sarah's 5 star reviews and her clients' radiant smiles are a testament to her extensive experience and passion for enhancing natural beauty. Whether she's crafting natural balayages or diving into edgy cuts, Sarah's creativity knows no bounds, and her clients trust her to deliver stunning results every time.

Now, as **Katie and Sarah** make the leap from **Base Hairdressing** to **Jakata Salon**, their journey continues with new challenges and opportunities for growth. But they are not strangers to Jakata, having trained and worked onsite during their apprenticeship, They are well-versed in providing exceptional customer care and ensuring a delightful experience for every client. And with an irresistible offer of **[25% OFF](https://www.jakatasalon.co.uk/offers/katiesarah)** for any new clients, Katie and Sarah are poised to make a splash at **Jakata**, bringing their unique talents and shining personalities to the forefront.

To **Katie and Sarah**, we extend our heartfelt congratulations on how far they've come and the bright future that lies ahead. As they embark on this new chapter of their career, we have no doubt that they will continue to inspire and delight clients with their exceptional skills and unwavering passion for hairdressing.
