## Tape Extensions:
![Tape pictures](https://35fba853288929fc2c78-69cb25cc8c90ed23a3699fb2b2ac841c.ssl.cf5.rackcdn.com/PK%20Hair%20Extensions%20Blog/tape.png)

Thin **tape wefts** sandwich your natural hair between them, providing a seamless and natural look.

**Pros:**

- Quick application process.
- Suitable for various hair types, especially finer hair.
- Comfortable and lightweight.

## Nano Extensions:
![Nano pictures](https://35fba853288929fc2c78-69cb25cc8c90ed23a3699fb2b2ac841c.ssl.cf5.rackcdn.com/PK%20Hair%20Extensions%20Blog/nano.png)

Tiny, **virtually undetectable** bonds are applied close to the scalp, offering a **discreet and natural** appearance.

**Pros:**

- Minimal visibility.
- Ideal for fine hair.
- Flexibility in styling.

## Micro Extensions:
![Micro pictures](https://35fba853288929fc2c78-69cb25cc8c90ed23a3699fb2b2ac841c.ssl.cf5.rackcdn.com/PK%20Hair%20Extensions%20Blog/micro.png)

Small **individual bonds** are applied to your natural hair, providing a **versatile and customisable** solution.

Pros:

- Excellent for adding volume.
- Discreet and natural-looking.
- Versatile styling options.

## Micro Weave Extensions:
![Mirco Weave pictures](https://35fba853288929fc2c78-69cb25cc8c90ed23a3699fb2b2ac841c.ssl.cf5.rackcdn.com/PK%20Hair%20Extensions%20Blog/micro%20weave.png)

A combination of **micro bonds** and a **wefted base** create a secure and long-lasting application.

Pros:

- Suitable for various hair types.
- Comfortable and secure.
- Less stress on natural hair.

## Braided Weave Extensions:
![Braided Weave pictures](https://35fba853288929fc2c78-69cb25cc8c90ed23a3699fb2b2ac841c.ssl.cf5.rackcdn.com/PK%20Hair%20Extensions%20Blog/braided%20weave.png)

Extensions are **sewn** into a **tightly braided base**, offering **durability and flexibility**.

Pros:

- Ideal for thick and coarse hair.
- Long-lasting results.
- Versatile styling options.
